Overview

You are invited to join Teacher Becky’s English Summer Camp: Summer in the UK, running from 10–14 August 2026.

  • This online camp is designed for adult learners of English as a foreign language at around B1–B2 level, with approximately 6 students in each class.
  • Build confidence using English in everyday life through practical, engaging lessons based on real-world situations and Functional Skills.
  • Course topics include: Discover your town; Food, picnics and cafés; Days out; Hobbies and social life; and a final Summer Challenge.
